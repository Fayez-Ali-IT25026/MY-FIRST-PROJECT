MBSTU Orientation Event -

Welcome to the MBSTU Orientation Event! This event is organized to warmly welcome our new students and help them begin their university journey with confidence. During the orientation, students will learn about the university, academic rules, campus facilities, clubs, and student services. There will also be speeches from respected teachers and senior students, along with fun activities to help everyone get to know each other. The event aims to create a friendly environment where new students can make friends, ask questions, and feel comfortable in their new academic life. Join us and make the first day of your MBSTU journey memorable!
